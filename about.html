About Repair Node

• • • •

Repair Node was started with the idea in mind that electronics are one of the big investments in everyone's life. Next to a house and a car; computers, cell phones, laptops, and tablets are the next extensions of our livelihoods. So often these devices are damaged and thrown in the waste. At Repair Node, we want to see new life out of those devices, and the happy people who own them — and help the environment as well.

We love what we do. From cracked screens and failing batteries to complex diagnostics and board-level repairs, every device that comes through the door gets our full attention. We treat your devices like our own.

With over 10 years of practice, a bit of college engineering experience, a lifetime of interest, and a lot of love and passion, we've fixed hundreds, if not thousands, of devices. We've helped people reconnect with the electronics they depend on and we look forward to helping many more.

Our objectives

  • Quality Repairs
  • Quality Parts
  • Knowledgeable Service
  • Customer Service
  • Affordable Prices
